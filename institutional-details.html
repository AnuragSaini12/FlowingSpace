Latest From Work

Kala Kunj: Cultural Centre & Archives Building, Delhi

SSA’s winning entry at the National level competition for this prestigious project for the Delhi Government is based on principles of sustainability & green building.

An open-air stepped courtyard with several existing trees is the focus of the project. A 400 seat Auditorium, a modern library, an Art Gallery and a Cafeteria will make this a much-desired Cultural hub in the heart of Delhi’s Institutional area. An environmentally controlled block will preserve the precious & historic documents for the Department of Delhi Archives.
