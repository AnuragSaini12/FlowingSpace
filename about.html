Who We Are

Company Profile

Sabyasachi Sen & Associates was founded in New Delhi in 1995 as a forum of young Architects, Interiors and Urban Designers & are today one of the leading Architects in India. SSA’s repertoire includes Architecture, Interior Design, Urban Design & Industrial Architectutre.

‘With a portfolio of over 100 world-class design projects, 20 years of solid experience, top class team of Architects & Engineers and diversity of skills – we help our clients to achieve their goals’.
Design Studio

Unique Solution

We do not believe in prototypical solutions. We work on each & every project project with a fresh approach & new ideas based on the site, the microclimate, the client and their individual requirements. This makes our every project unique.

Sustainable Design

Our buildings are climatically compatible, environmental friendly and energy efficient. We strongly believe that an environmentally focused design will be the only answer to sustainable development in the future.

Client Driven Service

Our quality and timely service has won us several accolades & recognition, in terms of design competitions, awards & extensive coverage in leading architectural magazines. However our greatest reward has been our clients’ satisfaction.
